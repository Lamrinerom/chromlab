Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Chromlab specializes in a wide array of laboratory and medical equipment tailored for research institutions, healthcare facilities, diagnostic labs, and industrial applications. The product range encompasses centrifuges for sample separation, incubators for controlled temperature environments, spectrophotometers for precise molecular analysis, microscopes for detailed visualization, biological safety cabinets for contamination-free handling, laboratory refrigerators and freezers for sample preservation, glassware washers for efficient cleaning, viscometers and rheometers for fluid property measurement, fume hoods for safe chemical work, balances for accurate weighing, filtration systems for purification, and baths or circulators for temperature regulation. Additional categories include shakers, ovens, autoclaves, and testing instruments to support diverse scientific workflows.

Quality remains paramount at Chromlab, with all equipment sourced from certified manufacturers adhering to international standards such as ISO 9001, CE marking, and relevant regulatory guidelines for laboratory safety and performance. Each item undergoes rigorous pre-shipment inspections, including functionality tests, calibration verification, and compliance checks to guarantee accuracy, durability, and reliability. Chromlab maintains detailed documentation for traceability, enabling customers to validate certifications upon request. This commitment minimizes operational risks, ensures seamless integration into existing lab setups, and supports long-term performance under demanding conditions. Customers benefit from products constructed with robust materials like stainless steel, designed for corrosion resistance and energy efficiency, aligning with global best practices for scientific instrumentation.​

Chromlab employs a streamlined shipping process to deliver laboratory and medical equipment promptly and securely across India and select international destinations. Upon order confirmation, customers receive a tracking number within 24-48 hours, with standard domestic delivery timelines ranging from 3-7 business days depending on location and product availability. Bulk or oversized items, such as fume hoods or refrigerators, may require 7-14 days, including professional freight handling to prevent damage. Chromlab partners with established logistics providers experienced in scientific cargo, ensuring temperature-controlled transport where necessary for sensitive equipment like incubators or biological cabinets.

Packaging adheres to industry standards, utilizing anti-vibration foam, protective crates, and moisture barriers to safeguard instruments during transit. Delivery options include ground shipping for cost efficiency or express air freight for urgent needs, with real-time updates via email and an online portal. International shipments comply with customs regulations, including export documentation and duties estimation provided upfront. Chromlab offers flexible scheduling, such as site-specific delivery during lab off-hours, and white-glove installation for complex setups. In the event of delays, proactive communication and alternative solutions, like partial shipments, maintain project timelines. Post-delivery inspections are encouraged, with discrepancy resolution handled within 72 hours to uphold service excellence.

Chromlab extends comprehensive warranty and support services to ensure the longevity and optimal performance of all laboratory and medical equipment. Standard warranties range from 1-3 years, covering manufacturing defects, parts, and labor, with extended options available for critical instruments like spectrophotometers or centrifuges. Coverage includes on-site repairs within major cities in India, remote diagnostics via video support, and loaner equipment during downtime to minimize disruptions.

The technical support team, comprising certified engineers, operates Monday to Saturday with 24/7 emergency response for mission-critical failures. Services encompass installation guidance, annual calibration traceable to national standards, preventive maintenance contracts, and training sessions for safe operation. Chromlab maintains a nationwide service network, stocking common spares for rapid turnaround—typically 48-72 hours. For customized needs, validation protocols align with GLP/GMP requirements, including IQ/OQ/PQ documentation. Customers access support through a dedicated helpline, email, or client portal for service history and scheduling. This end-to-end approach reduces total ownership costs, enhances equipment uptime to over 98%, and fosters compliance in regulated environments. Regular firmware updates and performance audits further extend product life cycles.

Chromlab maintains a customer-centric policy for returns, refunds, and payments to facilitate hassle-free transactions. Returns are accepted within 14 days for unused equipment in original packaging, subject to a 10% restocking fee for non-defective items; defective products qualify for free returns and full refunds or replacements. Processing occurs within 7-10 business days upon receipt and inspection, with prepaid return shipping labels provided. Hazardous materials or customized orders follow specific protocols to ensure safety.

Payments are secured via multiple channels, including bank transfers, credit/debit cards, UPI, NEFT/RTGS, and letters of credit for institutional buyers. Bulk orders benefit from net-30 terms post-credit approval, with no hidden fees. All transactions comply with PCI DSS standards for data security. Refunds are issued to the original payment method, excluding shipping costs unless due to Chromlab error. Order cancellations before shipment incur no charges, while post-shipment adjustments prioritize customer satisfaction. This transparent framework builds trust, supports budgeting for research grants, and aligns with procurement norms in academic and healthcare sectors. Detailed invoices and GST compliance aid financial reconciliation.

Chromlab facilitates effortless contact through multiple channels for custom quotes, technical consultations, and tailored solutions. The website's quote form generates responses in 4 hours, detailing pricing, specs, and lead times for bespoke needs like custom fume hoods. A toll-free helpline (9 AM-7 PM IST) connects to product specialists, while email yields 2-hour acknowledgments.

Sales engineers conduct virtual demos or site visits, assessing lab layouts for optimal equipment selection—over 200 consultations monthly. For enterprises, dedicated account managers handle RFQs, integrating with SAP or Oracle. WhatsApp Business enables instant queries on stock or compatibility. Feedback loops refine offerings, with NPS scores above 90. This responsive infrastructure supports complex projects, from incubator arrays to full lab turnkeys, ensuring alignment with budgets and timelines.

Chromlab is a trusted supplier of quality laboratory and medical equipment for research, diagnostics, and healthcare.
