Ion Chromatograph CLIC-601

CLIC-601 Ion Chromatograph delivers precise ion analysis for anions and cations in water, pharma, and food samples. Featuring a high-pressure 6.0 MPa pump with low-pulse delivery and bipolar conductivity detection (≤0.002 nS/cm resolution), it ensures ultra-trace sensitivity down to 0.0005 µg/ml Cl⁻. The compact system includes built-in lithium battery for portable fieldwork, PEEK components for chemical resistance, and intelligent workstation for automated control. With <40 µL cell volume and 220V AC compatibility, it's ideal for Indian labs needing reliable, stable performance for drinking water testing, quality control, and research applications.

Application

The CLIC-601 Ion Chromatograph excels in environmental analysis for monitoring water quality parameters like fluoride, nitrate, chloride, and sulfate in drinking water, wastewater, and groundwater samples. In industrial quality control, it ensures compliance by detecting ionic impurities in pharmaceuticals, food products, and beverages, supporting purity testing and regulatory standards.
